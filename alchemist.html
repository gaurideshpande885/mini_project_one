The alchemist

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is a timeless novel that blends simplicity with profound wisdom, offering readers a story about dreams, destiny, and self‑discovery.
Paulo Coelho’s The Alchemist tells the story of Santiago, a young Andalusian shepherd who dreams of finding treasure hidden near the Egyptian pyramids.
What begins as a simple quest for material wealth quickly transforms into a journey of spiritual awakening. Along the way, Santiago encounters a gypsy, a mysterious king, and eventually an alchemist, each guiding him toward the realization that the true treasure lies not in gold but in understanding oneself and listening to the heart.
