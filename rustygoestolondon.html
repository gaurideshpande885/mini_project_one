Rusty goes to London

Rusty Goes to London by Ruskin Bond is a charming continuation of Rusty’s adventures, this time taking him beyond the familiar hills of India to the bustling streets of London. The book captures Rusty’s sense of wonder and curiosity as he navigates a new city, meeting people, facing challenges, and discovering himself in the process. Bond’s trademark simplicity and warmth shine through, making the story relatable and heartfelt. It’s less about the destination and more about Rusty’s journey of growth, independence, and the bittersweet experience of leaving home behind.
